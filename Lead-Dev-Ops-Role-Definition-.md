<th><strong>About the role</strong></th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p>A Lead DevOps works on a large, complex project or multiple smaller related projects leading teams automating the build of infrastructure platforms. They are responsible for underpinning good development processes including managing tools and testing environments, central code control, maintaining development standards and writing software that automates systems.</p>
<p>They review other people’s systems designs to make sure the most appropriate technology is selected, ensure efficient use of resources and integration of multiple systems and technology. They analyse current processes and identify opportunities to optimise processes and lead teams to deliver service improvements. They explore the benefits of cross-government alignment of systems and services and provide mentoring and leadership to other DevOps.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><strong>What you’ll do day-to-day</strong></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><ul>
<li><p>You will be leading Dev Ops teams, writing code and managing the build process for infrastructure platforms.</p></li>
<li><p>You will collaborate with software developers to manage the build of pipeline for pushing out code, helping software to reach the business.</p></li>
<li><p>As the Lead DevOps, you will identify process optimisation improvements making sure that the organisation derives maximum value from services.</p></li>
<li><p>Acting as a technical product owner, you will be developing medium-long term strategies for product lines.</p></li>
<li><p>Using agreed security standards and specifications, you will design, create and test programmes or scripts of medium to high complexity.</p></li>
<li><p>Act as second line support, assisting the service desk team in directing issues to the application owner and responding to infrastructure platform issues.</p></li>
<li><p>You will be responsible for guiding the way in which the DevOps teams operates, sharing technical knowledge, keeping your colleagues upskilled with best practices and suggesting continuous improvements.</p></li>
<li><p>You will be part of the cross-government technical community, ensuring lessons learned and best practice advice is shared and representing the Home Office at cross-government meet ups.</p></li>
<li><p>You will play a key role in the development of the DDaT community, providing technical leadership, coaching and mentoring of the team, promoting knowledge sharing and adoption of good practice.</p></li>
<li><p>You will provide oversight to your DevOps teams working across 3 main areas; core platforms, BAU fixes and being the interface with the business,</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>You will be a coach and mentor to more junior DevOps colleagues supporting the development of their skills.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><strong>You will be someone who</strong></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><ul>
<li><p>Has strong technical leadership qualities; a passion for developing and implementing systems and the ability to inspire a culture of technical excellence and innovative software design.</p></li>
<li><p>Can navigate the complex landscape of technologies, stakeholders, contractors, internal and cross-government teams.</p></li>
<li><p>Is focused on business outcomes.</p></li>
<li><p>Can demonstrate great partnership skills, particularly the ability to build strong, effective partnerships with peers across the technology organisation.</p></li>
<li><p>Has good communication skills, verbal and written, and a good understanding of the use of different channels and formats for different audiences.</p></li>
<li><p>Is able to work with diverse teams across multiple locations and technologies.</p></li>
<li><p>Is able to anticipate and advise on future technology changes that present opportunities for the product or programme</p></li>
<li><p>Provides leadership to the teams responsible for delivery of the service through effective direction and coaching and mentoring of more junior colleagues.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><strong>You will have the following skills and experience</strong></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><ul>
<li><p>Recent and significant coding or scripting experience.</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to use a wide variety of open source technologies and tools.</p></li>
<li><p>An understanding of and ability to lead the technology team to adhere to good software design principles.</p></li>
<li><p>Experience of Agile development using continuous integration principles.</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to coordinate complex implementation activities across multiple systems and understand how to undertake and support testing activities.</p></li>
<li><p>Comfortable with the frequent, incremental code testing and deployment.</p></li>
<li><p>Strong grasp of automation tools.</p></li>
<li><p>Strong data management skills.</p></li>
<li><p>A proven background in technical leadership.</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to effectively communicate and collaborate with business and technology stakeholders, developers and users</p></li>
<li><p>A strong track record of managing and delivering complex systems within time, cost and quality targets.</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to define and manage the technical roadmap and strategy.</p></li>
</ul></td>
