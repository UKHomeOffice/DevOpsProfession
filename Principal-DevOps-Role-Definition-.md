<th><strong>About the role</strong></th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p>The Principal DevOps leads large or multiple teams in writing code to automate and manage the build of the pipeline to push out code. Their role is to define the strategic vision for delivery of the design of software and systems.</p>
<p>They frequently collaborate with and take part in planning with senior business stakeholders to ensure that security, stability and capacity are embedded in the development and deployment of services. A Principal DevOps is accountable for identifying and exploring opportunities for service and business improvement.</p>
<p>The Principal DevOps is the most experienced DevOps on the team so would be expected to advise on building the platform using specific technical knowledge of tools and methods.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><strong>What you’ll do day-to-day</strong></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><ul>
<li><p>As the Principal DevOps you will be analysing current processes, identifying and implementing opportunities to optimise processes, and leading and developing a team of experts to deliver service improvements. This will involve you evaluating and establishing requirements for the implementation of changes by setting policy and standards.</p></li>
<li><p>You will manage a group of DevOps automating the process of building new or amended infrastructure.</p></li>
<li><p>You will be responsible for using agreed security standards and specifications, to design, test and document new or amended software.</p></li>
<li><p>Lead you teams who act as second line support, assisting the service desk team in directing issues to the application owner and responding to infrastructure platform issues.</p></li>
<li><p>You will provide technical expertise when required for your teams to design, code and test programmes or scripts of medium to high complexity, using the appropriate standards and tools.</p></li>
<li><p>You will work with business and technology stakeholders to translate business problems into technical designs.</p></li>
<li><p>As an expert in many technical areas or a very deep knowledge in a particular technical area, you will recommend technologies or improvements to support and enhance the businesses’ goals. You will be required to present these recommendations clearly and understandably to senior management.</p></li>
<li><p>You will develop the internal capability by inspiring teams, coaching and mentoring more junior colleagues, recruiting strong technical talent and making sure the team proactively collaborate on projects.</p></li>
<li><p>You will design the roadmap and overall strategy for your area, and work with senior business stakeholders to implement this across DDaT.</p></li>
<li><p>You will be expected to engage with and support teams and use your technical expertise to advise and guide them.</p></li>
<li><p>Keep up to speed with the technologies outside of the Home Office to ensure continuous improvement in methods, standards and approach.</p></li>
<li><p>You will be part of the cross-government technical community, ensuring lessons learned and best practice advice is shared and representing the Home Office at cross-government meet ups.</p></li>
<li><p>You will play a key role in the development of the DDaT community, providing technical leadership, coaching and mentoring of the team, promoting knowledge sharing and adoption of good practice.</p></li>
<li><p>You will provide oversight to your DevOps teams working across 3 main areas; core platforms, BAU fixes and being the interface with the business.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><strong>You will be someone who</strong></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><ul>
<li><p>Has strong technical leadership qualities; a passion for implementing systems and the ability to inspire a culture of technical excellence and innovative solution design.</p></li>
<li><p>Is focused on business outcomes and moving the business forward and giving it a stronger technological advantage.</p></li>
<li><p>Can navigate the complex landscape of technologies, 3<sup>rd</sup> party suppliers, internal and cross-government teams.</p></li>
<li><p>Can demonstrate great partnership skills, particularly the ability to build strong, effective partnerships with peers across the technology organisation.</p></li>
<li><p>Has good communication skills, verbal and written, and a good understanding of the use of different channels and formats for different audiences.</p></li>
<li><p>Is able to work with diverse teams across multiple locations and technologies.</p></li>
<li><p>Provides leadership to the teams responsible for delivery of the service through effective direction and coaching.</p></li>
<li><p>Has good awareness of the other professions within DDaT, particularly amongst your peers so that you can engage effectively with the organisation.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><strong>You will have the following skills and experience</strong></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><ul>
<li><p>A deep expertise in a wide range of technical systems.</p></li>
<li><p>Recent and significant coding or scripting experience.</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to use a wide variety of open source technologies and automation tools.</p></li>
<li><p>Comfortable with fast coding, testing and deployment.</p></li>
<li><p>Strong data management skills.</p></li>
<li><p>Natural ability to lead the technology team to adhere to good software design principles from proposal through to implementation.</p></li>
<li><p>Experience of Agile development using continuous integration principles.</p></li>
<li><p>Able to coordinate large and complex activities across multiple systems and understand how to undertake testing activities.</p></li>
<li><p>Has a proven background in motivating fellow team members to achieve the shared objectives and push beyond their defined areas of competence.</p></li>
<li><p>A strong track record of managing and delivering complex systems within time, cost and quality targets.</p></li>
<li><p>You may be expected to manage 3<sup>rd</sup> party suppliers through all procurement phases.</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to effectively communicate and collaborate with business and technology stakeholders, developers and users.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
